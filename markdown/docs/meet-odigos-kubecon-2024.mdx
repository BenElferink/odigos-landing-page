We’re excited to announce that Odigos will be attending **KubeCon + CloudNativeCon North America** this November! 

**Date**: November 12-15, 2024

**Location**: Salt Palace Convention Center

**Booth**: P6 (between Hall 1 and Hall 2, Level 1—near the Learning Lounge and F&B & Seating)

Discover how Odigos simplifies observability with **zero-code instrumentation**—cutting setup time and manual effort to zero! Experience our solution that offers:

- **Less than 1% performance overhead**
- **Better coverage** with support for compiled languages
- **Accurate distributed tracing** with **higher-grade signals**
- **Reduced complexity** when instrumenting distributed systems

Whether you’re looking to dive deep into distributed tracing, application profiling, or eBPF-based observability, our team will be on-site to showcase the latest innovations. Stop by Booth P6 to see how Odigos can transform your observability approach!

**RSVP now** to schedule a meeting with us at the booth. We can’t wait to connect with you at **KubeCon + CloudNativeCon**!
